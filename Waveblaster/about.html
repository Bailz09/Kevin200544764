OUR COMPANY

RESTORATION

Yamaha Retro Sports is is a renowned restoration company dedicated to the revival and preservation of classic Yamaha motorsports vehicles. With a team of skilled craftsmen and engineers, the company meticulously restores and enhances these timeless machines to their former glory, ensuring that each component meets the highest quality standards. Their unwavering commitment to excellence is demonstrated through the use of genuine Yamaha parts and state-of-the-art technology, which allows these cherished vehicles to perform as they did in their heyday.

OUR HISTORY

HUMBLE BEGINNINGS

Yamaha Retro Sports started as a humble backyard operation, founded by two passionate enthusiasts who shared a deep appreciation for classic Yamaha motorsports vehicles. Driven by their love for these machines and a desire to preserve their heritage, the founders began by meticulously restoring a single, dilapidated Yamaha motorcycle. Word of their impeccable craftsmanship quickly spread, and the company has since grown into a respected enterprise.
